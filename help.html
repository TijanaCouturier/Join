Kanban method

With the Kanban method, a team controls its processes or tasks. To do this, the team uses a board (Kanban, Japanese: card, signal card) on which columns visualize the processing status of a task and the individual tasks wander across the board on maps. A Kanban board has at least three columns: Open (To Do), In progress (Doing) and Done (Done).
